# Copy Direction

## Brand Voice

The copy should feel polished, calm, discreet, and confident. It should speak to clients who own high-value homes, vacation homes, second homes, or properties that need consistent recurring care.

Use language that suggests:

- Trust.
- Discretion.
- Consistency.
- Local accountability.
- Detailed property care.
- Professional standards.

Avoid language that sounds casual, bargain-focused, or generic.

## Required Messaging

Use these ideas across the site where relevant:

- Insured and bonded.
- Locally owned and operated in Taos.
- Trained team.
- Background-checked team members.
- Recurring home care.
- Vacation-home and second-home care.
- Monthly home check-ins.
- Detailed first clean.
- Private walkthrough.
- Tailored quote.
- Rates start at `$65 per labor hour`.

## Phrases And Tone To Avoid

Avoid:

- "Make your place shine."
- "Sparkling clean" as a main value proposition.
- "Cheap", "affordable", "budget", or similar price-first wording.
- Overpromising language such as "perfect every time."
- Generic cleaning-service filler.
- Copy that sounds like a one-person casual side business.

## Page Copy Direction

### Homepage

Lead with premium property care:

- "Discreet, recurring cleaning and home care for Taos residences, vacation homes, and second homes."
- Emphasize peace of mind for clients who are away from their property.
- Make the CTA about a private walkthrough or consultation.

### Services

Make service descriptions specific and scannable.

Suggested categories:

- Recurring residential cleaning.
- Vacation-home care.
- Monthly home check-ins.
- Detailed first clean.

Include pricing as a professional starting point:

- "Rates start at $65 per labor hour. Final pricing is tailored after a walkthrough and may vary based on property size, location, condition, access, and service frequency."

### About

Keep Hannah's personal credibility but make it polished.

Suggested points:

- Hannah Beck has called Taos home for years.
- Taos Best Cleaning has grown from a one-person operation into a small trained team.
- Team members are background-checked and trained to consistent standards.
- The company is insured and bonded.
- The business is built around reliable recurring care.

### Contact

Make the next step feel private and professional.

Ask clients to include:

- Property type.
- Property location.
- Desired frequency.
- Whether the home is full-time, vacation, or second residence.
- Access details or special requirements.

Keep contact methods simple:

- `info@TaosBestCleaning.com`
- `575-613-2296`

### Employment

Keep concise and practical. This page should not compete with client-facing sales content.

### Availability

If retained, present availability as secondary support content, not the primary booking flow.
