# Luxury Redesign Plan

## Summary

Redesign Taos Best Cleaning for high-end residential, vacation-home, and second-home clients in the Taos area. The site should feel polished, discreet, trustworthy, and appropriate for clients who care about property care, consistency, privacy, and professional service.

The site must remain a static GitHub Pages site with no build step.

## Approved Direction

- Positioning: luxury homes, vacation homes, second homes, and other high-end residential clients.
- Logo: create a refined premium `TBC` monogram-style SVG logo with Taos mountain cues and subtle cleaning symbolism.
- Site structure: polished multi-page static website.
- Navigation: Home, About, Services, Contact in the primary nav.
- Employment: move out of the primary nav and into the footer.
- Pricing: show rates starting at `$65 per labor hour`.
- Conversion path: consultation/private walkthrough CTA using phone and email only.

## Primary Redesign Goals

- Replace the casual existing presentation with a premium professional format.
- Make the first viewport immediately communicate high-end home care in Taos.
- Build confidence through trust signals: insured, bonded, locally operated, trained and background-checked team.
- Explain recurring care, vacation-home care, home check-ins, and detailed first cleans clearly.
- Keep the site fast, static, simple to maintain, and compatible with GitHub Pages.

## Non-Goals

- Do not add a backend, contact form processor, scheduler, CMS, package manager, or framework.
- Do not make the site feel like a budget cleaning service.
- Do not keep Employment as a top-level buyer-facing navigation item.
- Do not leave inconsistent pricing or visible encoding issues.
