About

Local stewardship, dependable systems, and care designed for homes that matter.

Taos Best Cleaning serves homeowners who want a consistent local team, professional communication, and respectful attention to the details that keep a Taos home ready.

Hannah Beck built the company around trust, consistency, and local accountability.

Hannah has called Taos home for years. What began as a one-person operation has grown into a small, trained team that supports recurring home care for full-time residences, vacation homes, and second homes throughout the area.

Taos Best Cleaning is locally owned and operated, insured and bonded, and structured for clients who value privacy, dependability, and thoughtful property care. Team members are background-checked and trained to consistent service standards before entering client homes.

The company focuses on detailed first cleans, recurring maintenance, and monthly home check-ins that help owners stay confident in the condition of their property whether they are in Taos or away.
