Hi, I'm Hannah

I’m Hannah Beck, and I’ve proudly called Taos home for over eight years. What started as a one-woman operation has grown into Taos Best Cleaning LLC, a small team of reliable cleaners who share my commitment to thorough, consistent service.

We’re a locally owned and operated business, and every team member is background-checked and trained by me to meet the same high standards. As a company, we are fully insured and bonded, so you can feel confident knowing your home is protected. We focus on maintaining homes with recurring cleanings, starting with a detailed first clean and continuing with regular upkeep that keeps your space feeling fresh, comfortable, and welcoming.

If you’re looking for a small, trustworthy team you can rely on, I’d love to chat. Schedule a free consultation today, and let’s find the right plan for your home.
